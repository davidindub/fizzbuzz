How to Play

Counting upwards from 1...

If the number is divisible by three choose Fizz.
If it is divisible by five choose Buzz.
If it is divisible by fifteen choose FizzBuzz.
Otherwise select the Number.


You only have five seconds to answer!


In Hard Mode numbers are non-sequential.

You can click, tap, or use your keyboard's arrow keys to play:

  • Number
  • Fizz
  • Buzz
  • FizzBuzz
